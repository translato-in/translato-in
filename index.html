Frequently asked questions

How does the Translation process work?

Translato document translation is a fairly simple and secure process – after you are registered as a user, you simply have to submit your translation request (along with the source document) online through the form on our website (translato.in). We will assess volume, complexity and formatting requirements and email you a quote within 03 hrs. of your request. Once you approve the quote, we will immediately process the document for translation. The translated document will be emailed to you within 24 hrs. of your initial request.

Where can I submit a request for Translation?

For now, you can submit your Translation requests through the online form on our website (translato.in). We are in the process of rolling out this service through other means such as WhatsApp. We shall keep all our users notified about the latest.

What is the accuracy of your translation service?

We have achieved a high accuracy (99.9%) including preservation of context in our translations. This has been tried and tested over a variety of official documents.

Which all regional Indian languages do you support for translation?

For now, the translation service is active for Hindi-to-English translations. We are expanding this support for a variety of other Indian regional languages soon. We shall keep all our users notified about the latest.

Which types of documents do you translate?

We translate a variety of official documents. These include Contracts & Agreements, Court & Litigation documents such as Affidavits and Judgments, Immigration & Civil documents, Patents & IP, among others.

How long does it take for translation of my submitted document?

The translation of the submitted document takes a maximum of 24 hrs. The first step is to assess the submitted document after which we share the observations and a quote for your approval (emailed to you within 03 hours of the translation request). Once you approve the quote, we process the document immediately.

How do I track the status of my translation request?

We proactively notify you about the status of your translation through email. However, you can also check the status of your translation request through our online “Status Check” feature on our website.

How do you ensure the privacy and secrecy of the documents that I submit?

We take document privacy seriously. All submitted files are stored securely, handled under strict confidentiality, and deleted upon request. Access is limited only to authorized personnel for translation processing.

How to redress translation errors in the translated document that I received?

If you have questions or complaints about errors in the translated document, please raise a query through the online form available on our website. We will resolve your issue within 04 hours.

What are the payment mode(s) for your service, and when is the payment to be made?

UPI is the mode of payment for the service. We share the UPI link over email when the translated document is ready. The user is required to make the payment before the document is shared on the registered email.

Do you offer any bulk translation service?

Yes, we offer bulk translation services for select corporate, public, or private enterprises. For specific requirements, please email us at info@translato.in along with your contact number. We will reach out to you soon.

How do I access a list of all my translated documents that I have submitted for translation?

You can view all your previously submitted and completed translation requests by logging into your Translato user dashboard on our website.

Do you also provide a summary of the translated document, and what is the cost for the same?

Yes, as an optional service we provide a summary of the translated document. This option is available when you request the translation, and the cost depends on document length.

What input document formats do you accept, and any recommended format?

We accept input documents in PDFs, DOC/DOCX, JPG, and PNG formats. For best results, we recommend text-editable PDF or DOC/DOCX files.

Can I upload multiple documents in one Translation request and is there any size limit?

Yes, you can upload up to 10 documents in a single translation request. Each document can be up to 150 MB in size.

Do you follow any standards for translation of legal documents?

Our service aligns with ISO 17100 — the internationally recognized quality management standard for translation services. Our AI translation combines advanced technology with established global standards to ensure professional and reliable outputs.
